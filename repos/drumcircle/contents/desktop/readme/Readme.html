How to play

  • Drag from the tray to make a note.

  • Drag back into the tray to delete a note.

  • Place the mouse over a tile and mousewheel to pitch shift; or, middle-click and drag (option-click on mac)

  • Right-click and drag to duplicate a note (shift-click on mac)

  • Click the button in the center to pause.

  • Drag the crossbar to change tempo.

  • Tap the arrow in the bottom right to see more samples.

  • Hit the button in the upper right to change musical key.
