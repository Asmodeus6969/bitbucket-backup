in this exercise you will be presented with a galaxy of letters. you use the arrow keys to steer. the letters will shine with varying brightness, depending on how likely the program thinks the next letter is to be correct within an english sentence. your goal is to form one sentence. your score is how happy you are with the sentence. in this simulation you use the arrow keys to fly through a galaxy of letters. the program has what is called a markov chain statistical model of language, which allows it to guess how correct each letter is in the grammar of english. you will be guided toward the letters that most resemble english. the game ends when you have completed one sentence.
